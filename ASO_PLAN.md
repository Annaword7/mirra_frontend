## 🇬🇧 English

**Title (30):**
`Mirra: INCI Ingredient Analyzer`

**Subtitle (30):**
`Scan Cosmetics & Check Safety`

**Keywords (100):**
`skincare,beauty,skin,cream,serum,safe,toxic,care,decoder,database,avoid,bad,face,review,analyse,bulk`

**Promotional Text (144):**
`Decode any cosmetic's INCI instantly. Spot toxic & bad ingredients, allergens, and comedogenics — your #1 skincare ingredient checker. Try free!`

**Description:**
Mirra is the skincare ingredients checker app that decodes any cosmetic's INCI list in seconds — no chemistry degree required.

**Decode INCI. Instantly.**
Point your camera at any cream, serum, toner, or makeup label. Mirra's cosmetic scanner reads the ingredient list and runs it through our cosmetic ingredient database of thousands of compounds. You get a clear safety score, a plain-English verdict, and a full breakdown of every cosmetic active ingredient — so you always know what's in your skincare.

**Check ingredients in cosmetics that matter**
Mirra is more than a cosmetic checker. It flags:
• Allergens — contact sensitizers recognized by EU dermatologists
• Irritants — cosmetic ingredients to avoid for sensitive skin
• Comedogenics — pore-cloggers linked to breakouts
• Toxic ingredients in cosmetics — parabens, sulfates, formaldehyde releasers
• Bad ingredients in cosmetics — preservatives and additives with questionable safety profiles

Scan once, and you'll never wonder again whether your moisturiser is hiding something harmful.

**Your personal cosmetic ingredient database**
Every scan is saved to your library. Browse by brand, skin type, or safety score. Want to bulk check cosmetic ingredients across your entire shelf? Analyse cosmetics one after another — results never get lost.

Each product also shows a cosmetic ingredient review from the Mirra community — see what other users think about the formula before you buy.

**Made for your skin type**
Mirra matches every scan against your skin profile. Oily, dry, combination, or sensitive — see at a glance whether this product's cosmetic ingredients to avoid overlap with your personal triggers.

**PRO — go deeper**
• Full INCI cosmetics list with per-ingredient detail and sourced safety ratings
• Extended Key Facts: up to 4 issues + 5 top cosmetic active ingredients highlighted
• Unlimited scans
• Priority access to new features

Mirra supports English, Spanish, and Russian.

Download free. Scan your first product in under 30 seconds.
