## 🇬🇧 English

**Title (30):**
`Mirra: INCI Ingredient Analyzer`

**Subtitle (30):**
`Scan Cosmetics & Check Safety`

**Keywords (100):**
`inci,skincare,cosmetic,ingredient,scanner,beauty,skin,cream,serum,checker,safe,toxic,formula,care`

**Description:**
Mirra scans any cosmetic product and instantly decodes its INCI ingredient list. Find out if your skincare is safe, spot hidden allergens, irritants, and comedogenic ingredients before they harm your skin.

**What Mirra does:**
• Scan any cream, serum, toner, or makeup with your camera
• Get an instant safety score and verdict
• See Key Facts: allergens, irritants, comedogenic ingredients
• Explore top-rated products from the community
• Save scans to personal collections

**PRO features:**
• Full INCI ingredient list with detailed analysis
• Extended Key Facts (up to 4 issues + 5 top ingredients)
• Unlimited scans

Mirra supports English, Spanish, and Russian.
